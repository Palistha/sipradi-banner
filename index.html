Contractor Meet and Interaction Programme

JULY 20,2017

Sipradi Trading Pvt Ltd today signed the sponsorship agreements with U-19 cricketers Aarif Sheikh and Sunil Dhamala.As per the deal, Sheikh and Dhamala will receive Rs 20,000 each per month for two years.Director of Sipradi Trading P Ltd Shambhu Prasad Dahal handed over the contract papers to Sheikh and Dhamala amidst a programme today.Dahal informed that the company would also provide the players with cricketing kits and other supports in near future. “This is our first involvement in sports and we are determined for their success,” said Dahal.The players said such supports inspire them to do better in the field. “I will try my best to put in my best in future,” said allrounder Sheikh, who represented Nepal in the ICC U-19 World Cup held in Bangladesh this year and played all six matches. Sheikh, the top order batsman and medium pace bowler, made his national team debut last year against Namibia.
